Career Jump 1.6.6 fixes company filtering in the job inventory views.

This patch makes the company dropdown filters work correctly in both Available Jobs and Applied Jobs, and forces browsers to load the newest app script after deployment.

What's improved

- Selecting one or more companies now sends the correct company filter to the backend.
- Available Jobs and Applied Jobs now narrow the displayed list when company filters are applied.
- The app script is versioned in the page URL so stale browser-cached JavaScript does not keep old filter behavior alive after a release.

Why this matters

- Company filtering is one of the fastest ways to review a large inventory, especially after a fresh scan.
- The visible release version, API version, and deployed frontend version are aligned again.

Operational notes

- No database migration is required.
- No scan, ATS, notification, or inventory persistence behavior changed.
